What you’ll do

The Coramino Live Lounge is my baby. It’s a labour of love and a testament to my hard work because it brings together my two greatest passions – comedy and tequila. (Besides my family, of course. Not trying to get in trouble here.) The vibe is incredible – it’s old-school Hollywood meets Kevin Hart. Like a Rat Pack spot if there were more menu options than just Beef Wellington. Come join me and share a few laughs (and a few drinks).

Here’s the Kevin Hart-level VIP treatment you can expect.
  • When you enter through the velvet rope, you’ll be greeted with Gran Coramino’s finest – cristalinos, añejos and my personal favourite, reposados – all paired with the perfect plant-based appetiser. Just a quick heads-up – I will say “flavour profile” no less than fifteen times. I apologise in advance.

  • Did you even come if you didn’t snap a pic? Strike a pose for our photographer and let the ’gram know I’m taller in person, HA!
